Keynote Speaker



Robert Fraser, PhD
President and Chief Executive Officer, Molecular You

Abstract:

There is a revolution in healthcare in which health decisions will be preventive, data-driven and personalized. Biomarker analysis gives us health insights like never before and allows us to take proactive measures to manage health.

Visual analytics will play a key role in turning the vast amounts of health information into understandable, actionable insights. Molecular You is a digital health company developing an AI-driven playbook for personalized health to guide individuals to reach optimal health. The company has employed several visual analytic tools in its digital health platform to make the dynamic health information easy to understand for consumers and health practitioners.
