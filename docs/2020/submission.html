Submission

Authors can make submissions for (1) paper, (2) poster, or (3) demonstration.

  1. Paper: submissions are limited to eight pages.
  2. Poster: abstract submissions are limited to two pages.
  3. Demonstration: abstract submissions are limited to two pages.

Blind submissions are not required.

Online Submission

All material should be submitted via the EasyChair: https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=vahc2020

Submission Guidelines

  • Paper Formatting: For all submissions, please use the VGTC conference style template: http://junctionpublishing.org/vgtc/Tasks/camera.html

Review process:

Papers will be reviewed based on novelty, contribution, and relevance to the area of visualization, visual analytics, and health informatics. The program committee, which includes physicians and visualization experts, will review each submission and select a set of papers for paper, poster, or demonstration.

Contact vahc.sig at gmail.com if you have any questions.
